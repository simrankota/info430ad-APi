Create Intelligent Bots with the Azure Bot Service

Overview

One way to improve customer satisfaction is to help customers quickly locate answers to their questions. The information they're looking for might be in a frequently asked questions (FAQ) list, support documentation, or product manuals.

Search pages can help, but many organizations look for ways to interact more personally with customers while still using automation.

The QnA Maker service answers your users' natural language questions by matching them with the best possible answers from your knowledge base (KB). To find the information they need, customers can have an interactive and natural conversation with the automated service. You create a QnA Maker KB by using a web portal. In the portal, you can create, manage, train, and publish your service without any developer experience. After the service is published to an endpoint, a client application like a chatbot can use the service to process a conversation with a user to determine the question asked and respond with the best answer.
